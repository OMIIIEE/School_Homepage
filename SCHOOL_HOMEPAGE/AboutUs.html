About School

“in deum tendomud excellententiam”
Delhi Public School Ranchi embarked on its ‘navigationem ad consequi excellentiam” on 1st July 1989 in the SAIL Township of Ranchi which is marked for its sprawling lush greenery and fertile outlook. A co-educational English medium Senior Secondary School recognized by the Directorate of Education under All India 10+2 pattern in all science, commerce and humanities streams. It is scaling high under the aegis of the Delhi Public School Society, New Delhi; a brainchild of illustrious and distinguished educationists and philanthropists.

The school fosters motivated and academically curious global citizens by being a passionate, purposeful and progressive place to study. Our pupils transpire to achieve exceptional examination results in both academic and competitive categories and ensure entrance to a vast array of not only the country’s but the world’s most celebrated colleges/universities.

The school’s sociable and inclusive nature is characterised by the way in which pupils support each-other at daily peer-led events. They are upskilled by appreciating and learning from the knowledge and experience of those around them and to engage with current, political, scientific, cultural and academic developments within a safe and open environment.

Renowned as the pioneer of innovations in the educational ecosystem, DPS Ranchi is a highly academic school where individual and institutional excellence are strived for and encouraged in an atmosphere of open-minded enquiry without dogma, prejudice and unthinking conformity.
